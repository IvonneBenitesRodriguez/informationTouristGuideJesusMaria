Where to buy my peruvian chip?

You can buy your peruvian chip on the Real Plaza Salaverry Mall. Inside you will find 2 stores: Movistar and Claro.
To buy a chip you need to have in mind that:

    1. Both stores opens at 10am
    2. Bring your original passport
    3. A chip costs aproximately 5 soles. Preferable pay in cash.
    4. To make a charge of internet credits to your phone. The prices is approximately 25 soles (per 2 weeks).

How to arrive there from the house?

Just walk 14 minutes from the house.
