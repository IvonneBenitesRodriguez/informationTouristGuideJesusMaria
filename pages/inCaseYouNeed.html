What can I do to avoid robberies at the street?

The following are our advises:

  • Avoid to walk in unsafe areas.
  • Try to use your phone just at the university, coffee shops, house, Mall, and apartment.
  • Try to walk outside with small cash just for your expenses.
  • Always pay in cash, so you can avoid card's clonation.
  • Get a photo of your passport in your phone. Keep your original passport at the house.
  • If you use a local bus, try to check your belongings always.
  • Once you get a taxi, sit back the taxi driver's seat.
  • It is preferable to use reliable taxi service.

How to open a bank account?

Between all our local banks, the Bank who does simplify you all this procedure is Scotiabank. Scotiabank is placed in Real Plaza Salaverry and in Centro Comercial San Felipe. Both places are located very close walking 7-minutes from your house or apartment. Here the documentation you need to open a bank account:

  • Original Passport.
  • Presentation Letter from the University UP as a student(you can ask for it to your international coordinator at the Up).
  • Receipt Payment of water or electricity (You can ask us to provide it).
  • The minimum amount to open is 150 dollars.

Opening hours: M - F (9am - 7pm). Sat (9.30am - 1pm)
